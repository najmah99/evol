About Telehealth

Technology has the potential to improve the quality of health care. And technology can make it easier for more people to get health care. Telehealth may offer ways to make health care more efficient, better coordinated and closer to home. You can go to a virtual visit anywhere — such as at home or in your car. And you don't need to travel to go to a virtual visit.
